Pizza Recipes

pizza

Enjoy the simplicity of a Classic Margherita Pizza – a thin, crispy crust adorned with sun-kissed tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella slices, and aromatic basil. Drizzle with olive oil and bake to golden perfection for an authentic taste of Italy. Buon Appetito!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Sun-kissed Tomato Sauce
  • Fresh Mozzarella Cheese, sliced
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • Extra-Virgin Olive Oil

Cooking Method

  1. Preheat your oven according to the instructions for your pizza dough. Typically, it's around 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread a layer of sun-kissed tomato sauce evenly over the pizza dough. Arrange the fresh mozzarella slices on top.
  4. Scatter fresh basil leaves over the pizza for a burst of flavor and aroma.
  5. Drizzle extra-virgin olive oil over the pizza to enhance the flavors and help achieve a golden crust.
  6. Transfer the pizza to a preheated oven and bake according to the dough instructions or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Once out of the oven, let the pizza cool for a minute, then slice and serve hot. Enjoy your homemade Classic Margherita Pizza!
